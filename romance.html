Picture this as our love-at-first-sight moment, where I'm about to recommend a curated selection of 10 romance books that have already stolen my heart

Ready to fall head over heels into a world of love and desire? Let the romantic odyssey commence! 💖

AMERICAN ROOMATE EXPERIMENT

In"The American Roommate Experiment" by Elena Armas, Rosie Graham, a former engineer turned romance writer, faces writer's block while working on her next book. When an accident renders her apartment unlivable, she seeks refuge at her friend's place until repairs are completed. Unexpectedly, her friend's charismatic cousin, Lucas, is also staying there. Rosie and Lucas decide to share the space, and Lucas proposes a creative idea: four pretend dates to spark inspiration for Rosie's new novel

THE SELECTION

The Selection" marks the initial installment of a captivating trilogy. Thirty-five girls compete for the chance to reside in the palace, catching Prince Maxon's eye and potentially becoming his queen. America Singer's reluctant participation adds a twist, torn between forbidden love and an unexpected journey that challenges her aspirations. Amidst rebellion threats, her tale unfolds, unraveling a destiny beyond her imaginings.

BETTER THAN THE MOVIES

In this rom-com celebrating all things romantic, Liz, a dreamy teen pining for her forever crush, Michael, tries to capture his attention for a dreamy prom date. Unexpectedly, she finds a reluctant friendship with the irritating yet attractive boy next door, Wes. As they plot to make Liz noticed by Michael, Liz discovers a surprising connection with Wes, challenging her beliefs about love and happily ever afters. Think Kasie West and Jenn Bennett vibes, with a twist of unexpected romance.

KISSES AND CROISSANTS

In this enchanting tale set in an elite summer ballet program in Paris, seventeen-year-old Mia is determined to land an audition with a top ballet company. Amidst her dance dreams, she encounters the charismatic French boy, Louis, who becomes her unexpected tour guide. Together, they unravel the mysteries of the captivating city, creating a perfect summer adventure akin to the romance of Love and Gelato, ideal for those yearning to be swept away in the magic of the City of Love.

XOXO

Young cello prodigy Jenny dreams of a prestigious music conservatory. A chance encounter with Jaewoo, a charismatic K-pop star, sparks a night of adventure. Months later, they reunite at an elite arts academy in South Korea, where their budding romance challenges Jenny's carefully planned life. A must-read for K-pop enthusiasts, this tale delves into the risks of love.

IT ENDS WITH US

Love sometimes comes from unexpected places and can bring both joy and pain. Lily, a hardworking woman from a small town, finds herself drawn to Ryle, a charismatic neurosurgeon with a complex personality. Their connection feels perfect until Ryle's aversion to relationships surfaces, raising questions about his past. Amidst this, Lily grapples with memories of Atlas, her first love, whose return jeopardizes her newfound love with Ryle..

MY MECHANICAL ROMANCE

In this rom-com, opposites collide as Bel, a reluctant engineering talent, crosses paths with Teo, the charismatic captain of the robotics club. Teo sees Bel's unique abilities and wants her on the team, but their contrasting personalities spark both attraction and conflict. As they navigate their differences, Bel questions if she truly fits into the world of STEM.

THINGS WE NEVER GOT OVER

Naomi Witt, fresh out of a toxic relationship, finds herself drawn to wealthy bar owner Knox Morgan. The novel delves into themes of healing a wounded heart, family dynamics, and the complexities of love, blending elements familiar to the romance genre. Score's exploration of sibling rivalry, adoption challenges, and the impact of wealth adds depth to the narrative. The book's success led to a sequel, "The Things We Hide from the Light," in the same setting with some recurring characters.

THE LOVE HYPOTHESIS

In her third year of pursuing a Ph.D., Olive Smith, a skeptic of lasting relationships, finds herself in a predicament trying to convince her best friend, Anh, that she's on the path to love. In a moment of panic, she kisses the nearest man, Adam Carlsen, a sharp-witted professor. To her surprise, Adam agrees to play along as her fake boyfriend. As their charade unfolds amidst a chaotic science conference, Olive finds unexpected support in Adam, leading to a romantic experiment that blurs the lines of reality.

BEACH READ

In this literary romance, a cynic writer of literary fiction, a bestselling romance author, find themselves in a summer-long challenge to break their writer's block. Despite being complete opposites, they strike a deal: Augustus will write a happy story, and January will craft a Great American Novel. Their escapades to overcome creative struggles promise an unconventional summer, where they'll finish their books and, they believe, avoid falling in love.
